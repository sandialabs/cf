Analyst Decision

Analyst Decision is a minitool to record analyst decisions and their characteristics (consequence, techniques maturity, description...).

Actions:

  • To add a group click on "Add Decision Group" button.
  • To add a decision click on "Add" in the table.
  • To open a decision, click on "Open" button in the table.
  • To view a decision, click on "View" button in the table.
  • To update a decision, click on "Edit" button in the table.
  • To delete a decision, click on "Delete" button in the table.
