QoI Planner

QoI Planner tool defines the Quantity of Interest planning elements. The QoI created will be available in the PIRT - QoI section.

Actions:

  • To add a quantity of interest click on "Add Quantity of Interest" button.
  • To view a quantity of interest, click on "View" button in the table.
  • To edit a quantity of interest click on "Edit" in the table.
  • To copy a quantity of interest, click on "Copy" button in the table.
  • To tag a quantity of interest, click on "Tag" button in the table.
  • To delete a quantity of interest, click on "Delete" button in the table.
